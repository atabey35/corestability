Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the Shop in the game and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're logged in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How does the perk system work?

Every 5 waves, you'll be offered a selection of perk cards. Choose wisely - some perks have synergies that unlock special abilities when combined!

What are the best upgrade priorities?

It depends on your playstyle, but generally Damage and Range are essential for early game, while HP and Defense become crucial in later waves.

How do I beat boss waves?

Save your active skills for boss waves. Combining Freeze with Rocket or Laser is particularly effective against bosses.

Is my progress saved?

Your permanent unlocks and achievements are saved locally. Session progress (gold, upgrades) resets each run as part of the roguelite design.
