Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Introduction

Core Stability ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game progress and achievements
  • Upgrade levels and unlocked perks
  • User preferences and settings
  • Purchase history

Information Collected by Third Parties

We use third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA for advertising)
  • IP address
  • Device information

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store

In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all payment information according to their privacy policy.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes. You can change this permission at any time in your device Settings.

How We Use Information

Information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Save your game progress and preferences
  • Display relevant advertisements (for free users)
  • Process in-app purchases

Data Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal game statistics. We recommend keeping regular device backups to preserve your data.

Children's Privacy

Our game is rated for ages 12+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or submission of personal data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings
  • Delete local game data by uninstalling the app
  • Request information about data practices

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
